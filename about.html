About Us

Mission

Our mission at The Pit is to unite car and bike enthusiasts through bi-monthly racing events that highlight South Africa's unique motoring identy - from stance competitions and Quantam showcases to Spinna le Gusheshe and s'khothane-inspired entertainment.

We aim to provide a safe, thrilling and community driven spave where local talent and automotive pride take center stage.

Vision

To become South Africa's ultimate celebration of street racing culture, where passion for speed, style and local creativity comes alive every second weekend.

Community Contributions

Platform for Local Talent

Providing opportunities for local racers, modifiers and performers to showcase their skills.

Youth Empowerment

Encouraging youth involvement in motorsport and automotive art to inspire positive engagement and reduce illegal street racing.

Cultural Celebration

Showcasing uniquely South African car culture, including stanced VW Polos, Quantams and S'khothane fashion.

Enterpreneurship Boost

Supporting small businesses through vendor stalls, food stands and merch sales at every event.

Charity Drives

Hosting occasional donation drives (clithes, food parcels) during events to give back to the local community.

The Pink Pit Programme

Designed to empower women by including more of them in the pit.
