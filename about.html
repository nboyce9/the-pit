About Us

Mission

Our mission at The Pit is to unite car and bike enthusiasts through bi-monthly racing events that highlight South Africa's unique motoring identy - from stance competitions and Quantam showcases to Spinna le Gusheshe and s'khothane-inspired entertainment.

We aim to provide a safe, thrilling and community driven spave where local talent and automotive pride take center stage.

Vision

To become South Africa's ultimate celebration of street racing culture, where passion for speed, style and local creativity comes alive every second weekend.
